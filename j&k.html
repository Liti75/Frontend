National Parks of Jammu & Kashmir

Jammu & Kashmir, a beautiful region in India, is home to a variety of wildlife and rich natural beauty. The region features several well-known national parks that are perfect for nature lovers and conservationists. Here are a few famous national parks:

  • Hemis National Park – A high-altitude park famous for its rich biodiversity, including the elusive Snow Leopard.
  • Dachigam National Park – Home to the endangered Hangul deer and rich vegetation, located near Srinagar.
  • Kishtwar National Park – A beautiful park situated in the Kishtwar district, known for its scenic landscapes and wildlife.
  • Kazinag National Park – A park located on the borders of Jammu & Kashmir and famous for its wildlife diversity.

These national parks are essential for preserving the natural heritage and biodiversity of Jammu & Kashmir. Visit them to explore the beauty of nature and contribute to conservation efforts.
